Your Guided Path to ESG Reporting

Your ESG journey begins with a comprehensive GAP Assessment. We benchmark your current policies, data, and operations—then conduct a Double Materiality Assessment to identify key impacts and opportunities, establishing which ESG topics are most significant for your business and stakeholders.

We help you define your reporting boundaries—establishing which legal entities, business units, and activities are in scope. At the same time, we advise on governance structures and roles, so responsibilities for ESG processes are clear and measurable, and every stakeholder is engaged.

We then guide you in choosing and capturing the right ESG Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)—from carbon emissions and resource use to governance and social metrics. Our approach ensures that you collect data specific to your company’s risks, opportunities, and industry standards, aligned to the KPIs identified in earlier assessments.

With the metrics defined, we support you in centralizing your ESG data, implementing controls for accuracy, and ensuring all information flows smoothly across teams. Our systems and quality checks make data collection reliable and audit-ready. Utilizing robust analytics tools, we can transform your data into actionable insights and visuals. Your ESG story becomes clear and compelling, supporting internal awareness and external trust with boards, investors, and regulators.

We help you choose the optimal reporting frameworks— from GRI and TCFD to local regulations—and prepare disclosures that meet both mandatory and voluntary requirements. This ensures you’re aligned with industry standards and regulatory expectations, ready for informed report creation.

Finally, we prepare your disclosures, data controls, and documentation for assurance readiness—from mock audits to control reviews—giving you confidence before external review. Rebalance Impact stands with you for ongoing improvements and updates as ESG requirements and expectations evolve.
